Vin, The Ascendant Warrior
In the years following the Final Ascension, she becomes a mythical figure to the descendants of the survivors, known as the Ascendant Warrior. In the Words of Founding, it is said that she became the guardian of the mists and now watches over people in the night.
Vin's Equipment
A mistcloak is a cloak worn while traveling through the mists
A handful of coins is often used for Steelpushing
Obsidian daggers to prevent Allomantic influence by her opponents
Being a Mistborn, Vin has the ability to access and burn all of the Allomantic metals.
"She was strong and vulnerable all at once. She was my last master, and my greatest. She had a way of pouring everything of herself into what she did. When she fought, she was the blade. When she loved, she was the kiss. In that regard, she was far more . . . human than any I have known."
-TenSoon
